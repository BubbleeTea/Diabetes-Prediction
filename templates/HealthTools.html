Health Tools

Glucometer

A glucometer, also known as a blood glucose meter, is a portable device used to measure the concentration of glucose (sugar) in the blood. It is commonly used by people with diabetes to monitor their blood sugar levels regularly.

Insulin pump

An insulin pump is a medical device designed to help manage diabetes by delivering a continuous and customizable supply of insulin to the body. It is primarily used by people with Type 1 diabetes, although some individuals with Type 2 diabetes who require insulin therapy may also use it.

Ketone test strips

Ketone test strips are small, paper strips that detect the presence of ketones in urine. Ketones are substances produced when the body breaks down fat for energy, and their presence in urine can indicate a state of ketosis, which is common in low-carb diets, fasting, or certain medical conditions like diabetes.

Blood Pressure Monitor

A Blood Pressure Monitor is a medical device used to measure a person's blood pressure. It consists of an inflatable cuff to collapse and then release the artery under the cuff in a controlled manner, and a mercury or aneroid manometer to measure the pressure. There are also digital versions available that use oscillometric measurements.
